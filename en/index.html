Beauty, wellbeing and relaxation

A place to take the time you deserve.

Estetica Luce is a space dedicated to beauty, wellbeing and relaxation. With a holistic and functional approach, every treatment is personalised to meet your aesthetic needs and nourish body and mind.

From facial care to regenerating body treatments, each service is designed with care to enhance your natural beauty and create balance between outer and inner wellbeing, with passion, attention and professionalism.

Tailored beauty paths

Personalised paths designed around every need of your body.

Face and body

Facial glow and body harmony

Every path begins with careful listening to the needs of the skin and body. Facial treatments bring radiance, freshness and daily care, while body protocols help restore lightness, harmony and a pleasant feeling of wellbeing.

We use Marzia Clinic products to support each treatment with professional formulas and targeted choices, creating a personalised path aligned with your aesthetic goal.

Hands and feet

Hands & Feet Architecture

We care for the elegance of your extremities with specific treatments. From classic manicure with nail polish and semi-permanent application to simple nail art, every detail gives your hands a refined and elegant touch.

Feet find their full beauty and relief through our specialised pedicure protocols. Hydration is always part of our attention, with intensive moisturising treatments designed to restore comfort and softness.

Wellbeing & Balance

Massages

Time dedicated to slowing down, releasing tension and reconnecting with your body. Our massages are personalised with essential oils, candle butter and enveloping manual techniques according to your needs.

We offer facial massages, decontracting and relaxing back massages, draining leg massages and paths with complete pressotherapy for a deep feeling of lightness and wellbeing.

Brows and lashes

Eye Harmony

The gaze gains character when every detail is balanced. We care for eyebrows with classic hair removal and shape definition, naturally and harmoniously framing the face.

To enhance lashes, we perform lash lamination treatments with InLei protocols, designed to highlight the natural curve and give the eyes a neat, luminous and refined effect.

Skin wellbeing

Hair Removal

Hair removal is a care gesture designed for skin wellbeing. We work with attention, precision and respect, choosing products suitable even for the most delicate skin.

We use titanium wax for sensitive skin, chosen to offer comfort during the treatment and leave the skin soft, even and pleasantly smooth.
